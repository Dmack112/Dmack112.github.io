Welcome to my Portfolio

The whole point of this portfolio is to showcase my abilities and demonstrate my skills. I am currently a teacher at Dover High School. I teach computer programming and the introductory engineering courses. I have many project ideas and an impressive amount of curriculum created for all my courses as I am always looking for the best way to improve my classes. I am in my 5th year of teaching here at Dover. I love problem solving and creating new solutions to solve any problem I encounter.


I run the Dover High Gaming Club; Students stay after in a social environment and play role-playing or board games. It's an extension of one of my hobbies. I am actively running several role-playing games, designing my own and I love just playing any and all board games. I have many hobbies like drawing, writing, hiking, baking, making my own sauces/marinades. I'm hoping one of these hobbies will evolve into something more when I retire from teaching, but for now its just fun to learn new things.

Feel free to contact me at any of my email addresses!
